Example: Video HD/SD

To watch this video, you need the latest Flash-Player and active javascript in your browser.

This is an example of how to include two different qualities of one video. Here you can switch between the two videos »nice-flowers-hd.mov« and »nice-flowers-sd.mov«. These two videos aren't very big to keep this .zip file small. It shouln't be a problem to include a video with 720p, for example. Try different sizes to find your perfect setup.
